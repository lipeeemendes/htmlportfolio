Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self-raisig flour

Notes on ingredients

Castor Sugar 
 Finely granulated white sugar
Self-raising flour 
 A pre-mixd combination of flour an leavening agents(usualy sal an baking powder)

Method

  1. Prechert the ovn to 190 C.
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium bownl, whip together the eggs and casor sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in Flour.
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in the precheated oven, or unti the top of the cake springs bck when lightly pressed.
  7. cool in the pan over a wire rack
